DESCRIPTION

CASE STUDY

Based on a previous published summarizer -- SubSum which will select the most important sentences from original text. This project intend to investigate its ability to adapt to languages other than English.

In this project, I was responsible to design programs for machine translation, automatic evaluation, and finally the GUI of the algorithm.

RESULT

The corresponding paper was accepted and published on IEEE ICSC 2015 conference
